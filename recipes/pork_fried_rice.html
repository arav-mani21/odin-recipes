Pork Fried Rice

Description

Pork fried rice is a quick and flavorful stir-fried dish combining seasoned pork, rice, vegetables, and eggs in a savory soy-based sauce. It’s a versatile meal that’s perfect for using leftover rice and customizable with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Season pork with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, then sauté until cooked.
  2. Lightly scramble eggs in a hot pan and set aside.
  3. Stir-fry vegetables in oil until tender.
  4. Add rice to the pan, breaking up clumps, and stir-fry with vegetables.
  5. Mix in cooked pork, eggs, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, stirring until evenly coated.
  6. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.
