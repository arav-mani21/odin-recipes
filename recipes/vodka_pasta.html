Vodka Sauce Pasta

Description

Vodka sauce pasta is a creamy and flavorful Italian-American dish featuring a rich tomato-based sauce enhanced with vodka, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It pairs perfectly with pasta, creating a comforting and indulgent meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Vodka
  • Heavy Cream
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then reserve some pasta water and drain.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and let simmer.
  4. Add vodka to the sauce and cook until reduced.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and red pepper flakes for a creamy texture.
  6. Toss cooked pasta with the sauce, adjust consistency with reserved pasta water, and serve topped with Parmesan.
